Certifications for students


More than 25,000 organisations in 130 countries around the world rely on our
secure exams and tests as proof of English language ability.
Our unique approach encourages continuous progression with a clear path to improve language skills.
We have qualifications for schools, general and higher education, and business.

 

Cambridge English: Starters

 This exam is for younger learners. It 
 helps younger children take their
 first steps towards learning English

 

Cambridge English: Movers

 This test introduces children to spoken
 and written English daily. It is an 
 excellent way for them to gain confi-
 dence and improve their level

 

Cambridge English: Flyers

 This test is created specifically for
 younger children. It strengthens their 
 confidence and lays the foundation for
 future success in English

 

Key English Test (KET)

 KET certification is an elementary exam
 that assesses the ability to work with 
 frequently used spoken and written text
 that make immediate reference to
 everyday life.

 

Preliminary English Test

 IT is an intermediate exam that assesses
 the ability to work with frequently used 
 spoken and written texts that refer to
 known situations.

 

First Certificate in English

 IT is an an intermediate-high level exam
 that assesses the ability to interact 
 spontaneously and fluently, clearly
 expressing views on various topics.

 

Certificate of Proficiency in English

 The CPE is a proficiency test for those
 students who already have an advanced
 level and communicate very well in
 virtually all situations

 

Certificate in Advanced English

 CAE is an advanced exam that assesses
 the ability to communicate in English a
 work and in education.
